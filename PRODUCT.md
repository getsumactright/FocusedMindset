# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Young adults (roughly 18-34) into fitness, hustle culture, and mental discipline — the "tunnel vision" crowd. They browse on their phone, often from an Instagram/TikTok link, deciding in seconds whether the brand looks legit enough to buy from. Job to be done: quickly understand what Focused Mindset (FCSD) sells, feel the brand's energy, and get to a product they want to buy.

## Product Purpose

A streetwear clothing brand (hats, tees, hoodie/jogger sets, track sets, shorts) built around a "stay focused" mindset — working out, staying healthy, mental clarity, chasing goals. The homepage exists to showcase the current drop with real product photography and get visitors to a purchase (currently fulfilled via the existing Shopify store). Success = visitors immediately read the brand as premium/legit streetwear, not a generic template store, and click through to a product.

## Brand Personality

Grind, discipline, tunnel-vision focus — three words: driven, gritty, sharp. Existing product photography already carries this: black backgrounds, smoke and spotlight, graffiti-drip lettering, brick-wall lifestyle shots, an angular "F" wing logo. Voice is direct and motivational (existing tagline: "Crafted for the ones who stay focused—built from the grind, made for the streets.").

## Anti-references

- The current placeholder site (soft sage/green gradient, rounded "wellness app" cards, Fraunces serif headings) — reads as generic SaaS/wellness template, not streetwear.
- Generic Shopify-default storefront look (plain white grid, default sans, no atmosphere).
- Confirmed direction: dark streetwear energy, but with ONE sharp modern accent color instead of literal red/gold-on-black merch-site clichГ©, so it reads as a designed brand site, not a print-on-demand catalog.

## Design Principles

1. Photography-led: the product shots (smoke, spotlight, brick, graffiti type) are the brand — design frames and amplifies them, never competes with busy backgrounds behind them.
2. One committed accent color carrying real weight, not a rainbow of "focus" pastels.
3. Motivational voice in short, punchy lines — no generic marketing filler ("Welcome to our store").
4. Every product card is a clear path to the real Shopify product page — this is a showcase-to-purchase funnel, not a closed demo.
5. Avoid every template tell: no tiny uppercase eyebrows over each section, no identical icon-card grids, no gradient text.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Standard WCAG AA: body text ≥4.5:1 contrast even against the dark theme, visible focus states, alt text on all product photography, respect prefers-reduced-motion.
